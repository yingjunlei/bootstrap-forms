THE AMAZING WORLD OF GUMBALL QUIZ

The Amazing World of Gumball is an animated comedy television series created by Ben Bocquelet for Cartoon Network featuring a wide range of primary and supporting characters. It surrounds the daily shenanigans of Gumball Watterson and his family and friends. It is currently on its 6th season and is considered as one of Cartoon Network's most popular series.
